About me

I am a Ph.D. student in The Kim Jaechul Graduate School of AI at Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST), advised by Prof. Kijung Shin at Data Mining Lab.

I received my M.S. in Statistics and Data Science and B.A. in Applied Statistics from Yonsei University, where I was advised by Prof. Seyoung Park.

My research interests lie at the intersection of statistics, machine learning, and data mining. Recently, I have also developed interests in large language models and agentic systems.
